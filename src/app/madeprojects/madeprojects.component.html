My projects

WORKO

is an app designed to help you follow your gym routines and workouts. It offers a wide variety of routines and exercises, adapting to different levels and training goals.

The application is developed using Spring Boot for the back-end. The back-end is composed of multiple Spring Boot instances connected to their respective databases and a Spring Facade that connects to the front-end and distributes data to the other Spring Boot services. The front-end is built with JSF (JavaServer Faces) . For data management, Worko utilizes four databases distributed between MongoDB and MySQL .

EntreCol+

is a web application designed for a company to comprehensively manage its employees and resources. It offers functionalities such as employee management, including salaries, pensions, updates, and EPS (Health Service Provider), as well as a system for managing entertainment inventories, like movies.

Additionally, it features various modules and interactive charts to facilitate the understanding and analysis of information. The application also includes a login system and an administrator account to ensure security and proper access. This combination of features makes EntreCol+ a robust and efficient tool for business administration.
